Upcoming Events

Note: Even though all age groups are welcome minors must be accompanied by an adult.

1. Fireworks over the lake

After the sun goes down, watch the sky light up over Thompson Bay with fellow boaters, beach-goers and tailgaters. Get to your viewing location early and celebrate the magic and wonder of this Fourth of July fireworks spectacular, or spend the day on the water and tie up with your fellow boaters in Thompson Bay to watch the fireworks from the comfort of your boat.

Admission Prices:

Free

Date/Time:

July 4th, 2022 @ 9:00pm - 9:15pm

Best Viewing Locations:

Rotary Park (Free parking, coolers allowed, no alcohol or pets permitted)

The Nautical Beachfront Resort ( Limited free parking,no pets permitted, coolers and outside alcohol not permitted)

London Bridge Beach (No pets permitted, free parking, coolers allowed)

Thompson Bay (Via boat*, please anchor both bow and stern due to winds and current, anchor early as Thompson Bay fills up)

2. Lake Havasu Holiday Boat Parade of Lights

Holiday lights will adorn boats as they glitter and glisten through the famed Bridgewater Channel under the London Bridge as a new organizer continues to bring the community a 37-year-old tradition!

Admission Prices:

Free

Date/Time:

December 3rd, 2022 @ 7:00pm - 9:00pm

Location:

Bridgewater Channel @ London Bridge 422 English Village

3. Southwest Kayaks Fishing Open Championship

Experience kayak bass fishing in this amazing open fishing tournament series featuring a $20 buy-in and a 100 percent payout! The tournaments are held the first Saturday of every month!

Admission Prices:

$20

Age:

Participating anglers must be 18 or older or accompanied by an adult.

Date/Time:

December 4th, 2021 @ 6:00am - 12:30pm

Location:

Mesquite Bay Central

4. 12th Annual Havasu Balloon Festival & Fair

You are cordially invited to the 12th Annual Havasu Balloon Festival & Fair designated as one of the TOP 100 EVENTS IN NORTH AMERICA by the American Bus Association -an event you do not want to miss!

Admission Prices:

$20( tickets are good for all 4 days)

Children 10 and younger are free

Date/Time:

January 19th - 22nd, 2023 @ 12:00pm - 10:30pm

Main Gate/Parking/Boat Parking Along the Channel

All event parking is free other than VIP parking

For those arriving by boat, there will be a separate gate entrance for boaters who park along the Bridgewater Channel and enter through the beach. All boat parking is free and unreserved.
